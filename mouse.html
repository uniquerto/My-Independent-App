Mice

Having a great mouse is an essential part to your PC setup, usually there are 2 main types of mice people use, the work ones, which tend to have a smooth plain design with only one color such as white or black. Then there are gaming mice, these mice tend to have unique attrabutes. Some attributes are higher DPI, this means that the sensitivity of your gamiming mice can go much higher compared to a regular mouse. Another is the design of the mice, gaming mice usually tend to look appealling with rgb lights and unique patterns.
